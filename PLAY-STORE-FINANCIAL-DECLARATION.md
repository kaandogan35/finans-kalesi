## Açıklama (İngilizce — Play Console'a yapıştır)

ParamGo is a bookkeeping and cash flow tracking tool designed for Turkish tradespeople and small-to-medium businesses (SMBs).

The app allows users to:
- Track post-dated checks (vadeli çekler) received and issued, with due dates and status
- Manage accounts receivable and payable (cari hesaplar)
- Record daily cash transactions (kasa hareketleri)
- Track payment schedules and overdue payments
- Generate financial reports (income, expense, balance summaries)

ParamGo does NOT:
- Process payments or transfers on behalf of users
- Provide investment advice or brokerage services
- Offer lending, credit, or insurance products
- Act as a financial intermediary or payment service provider

Regulatory note: ParamGo is a record-keeping and tracking tool only. It does not require BDDK (Banking Regulation and Supervision Agency) or CMB (Capital Markets Board) licensing under Turkish law, as it does not perform financial intermediary functions.
